Book Presentation

1984 by George Orwell (Mil novecientos ochenta y cuatro)

1984 is a dystopian novel written by George Orwell. The story takes place in a totalitarian society controlled by Big Brother. The novel explores themes such as surveillance, truth manipulation, and loss of freedom. You can read more about the novel on this webpage about 1984 .

I found this book very powerful because it shows how language and information can be used to control people. It makes readers reflect on modern society and the importance of critical thinking.

“Big Brother is watching you.” — George Orwell
